Hot Chocolate

the perfect drink for those winter months

Ingredients

  • milk
  • your favorite hot chocolate powder
  • ground cinnamon
  • ground nutmeg
  • ground cloves
  • ground ginger
  • ground ceyanne pepper

Steps

  1. heat up a cup of milk
  2. add chocolate mix to taste
  3. add 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  4. add 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  5. a dash of cloves
  6. a dash of ginger
  7. a dash of ceyanne pepper
  8. mix until homogenous
  9. enjoy
